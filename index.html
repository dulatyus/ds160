How I Filled Out the DS-160 (and How You Can Too)

When I was filling out the DS-160 US visa application for myself and my family, I noticed that some agencies charge up to $20–30 just to complete the form. But honestly, you can fill it out yourself — it's not hard if you have a step-by-step guide.

So I created this free helper to walk you through it clearly. I hope it saves you time, money, and stress. Cheers!

What is DS-160?

The DS-160 is an online non-immigrant visa application form required for US visitor visas (B1/B2), student visas (F, J), and others.

Helpful Tips

  • Use Latin characters only.
  • Save your application ID.
  • Prepare a passport-style photo before starting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to submit the form online?

Yes. DS-160 must be submitted online before scheduling a visa interview.

Can I edit after submission?

No. You must create a new form if corrections are needed.
